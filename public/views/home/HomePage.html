  About

 

Your pants are too tight; you can’t fit into your school uniform or suddenly your dress is too close-fitting for you. You take a pledge of eating healthy food and dropping a size. But how would you know which bad food habit to get rid of? Moreover, you don’t want to go through big articles about healthy food habits or spend a fortune and visit a nutritionist.

"Healthy Gobbler" is an online dietary tracker which helps users to track their daily calorie intake.

Features:

  • Search for food items and log your meals.
  • Know your recommended daily calorie intake.
  • Track and control your daily calorie intake.
  • Connect with other users of the application to share your experiences.
  • Add reviews and comments for food items you ate.

 
 

Why this is important?
Unhealthy eating habits have contributed to the obesity epidemic in the United States. About one-third of U.S. adults (33.8%) are obese and approximately 17% (or 12.5 million) of children and adolescents aged 2—19 years are obese. A poor diet is associated with major health risks that can cause illness. These include heart disease, hypertension (high blood pressure), type 2 diabetes, osteoporosis, and certain types of cancer. By making smart food choices, you can help protect yourself from these health problems.
